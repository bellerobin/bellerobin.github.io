Welcome to Belonging
An Archival Pre-wedding Shoot
crafted by Robin & Belle

As it turns out, we are a quite shy couple. Rather than depict our faces, we photographed things that we treasured.

Nowadays, pre-wedding shoots are mandatory rituals. We go to beautiful places, dressing up magnificently in front of cameras, seizing that soon-to-extinct moment right before starting a new phase of life. Yet, if pre-wedding is about that “before” moment, we thought that there should be more to it than just our timid faces.

Thus, as our pre-wedding shoots, we photographed our belongings, those meaningful things that we have kept and treasured but may later be forgotten or left out once we live together. These photographs are records of our past. They also tell our parallels—our similarities and differences.

But what is also beautiful about belonging, is that they are not only about possession, but also intimate relationships. They are where we belong, just as we hope where our relationship will be.
