Belonging:

An Archival Pre-wedding Shoot

crafted by Robin & Belle

Pre-wedding photo sessions have become mandatory rituals. We go to beautiful places with our beloved photographers, dressing up in front of the cameras, seizing that soon-to-extinct moment right before starting a new phase of life. Yet, if pre-wedding is about that “before” moment, we thought that there should be more to be captured than just our timid faces.

Thus, as our pre-wedding shoots, we photographed our belongings, those meaningful things that we have kept and treasured across time but may later be forgotten or left out once we live together. These photographs are records of not only our past, but also our parallels—our similarities and differences.

But what is also beautiful about belonging, is that they are not only about possession, but also intimate relationships. They are where we belong, just as we hope where our relationship will be.
